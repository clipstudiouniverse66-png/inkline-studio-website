About Inkline

The work resists optimisation.

Inkline Studio designs calm paperback journals and minimal Notion templates for people quietly tired of being measured by their tools.

The thesis is small: that the readers, the students, the working professionals, the people doing thoughtful work tend to be the ones who reach for paper. So we make paper worth reaching for — cream pages, navy serif typography, and pages that don't ask you to optimise yourself in order to use them.

No app pairings. No streaks. No motivational quotes. No “you’re behind on your goal” notifications. The journal sits on your shelf and waits.

Designed in the United Kingdom. Printed by Amazon KDP on cream paper.

For Teams

Bulk orders for working teams.

For teams of 25 or more — onboarding kits, year-end gifts, wellness perks, conference giveaways — we offer direct bulk pricing on the Calm Work Journal and the rest of the series.

Each book is shipped from the UK. Custom inserts available on orders of 100+. The pitch we make to your colleagues is the same pitch we make to everyone: a journal that doesn’t moralise at the people using it.
